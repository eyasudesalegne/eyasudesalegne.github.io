01 — About

Engineering at the intersection of intelligence, imaging, and health.

My research asks how advanced biomedical technologies can become more explainable, reproducible, and useful in real experimental settings.

I combine machine learning, optical imaging, computational neuroscience, and practical device development. Across these areas, I care about methods that can be tested, inspected, and improved—not merely impressive headline results.
