About

I am a PhD student in Biomedical Engineering at Istanbul University–Cerrahpaşa (IUC). My interests include EEG/BCI, closed-loop neurostimulation, and computational brain-network modeling for neuropsychiatric research.

Focus areas

  • Neural signal processing (EEG) and real-time pipelines
  • Closed-loop stimulation and control logic (simulation → experiments)
  • Network models and data-driven evaluation
  • Reproducible tooling and research-grade code

Research

EEG/BCI

Preprocessing, artifact handling, features/embeddings, classification, and latency-aware design.

Closed-loop neurostimulation

State estimation, control rules, evaluation metrics, and simulation-based feasibility.

Computational modeling

Brain-network dynamics and hypothesis-driven simulation for neuropsychiatric applications.
